## **Notes** 
---
Kingscote Lake is at the southern end of Algonquin Park. The area gained protection due to the famous Kingscote "silver" lake trout.

Scorch Lake Mountain can be accessed from the southeast portion of Scorch Lake and possesses a viewing platform after a 1 km hike/climb.

The trip could be done in 2 days, but having an extra day to hike Scorch Lake Mountain would be preferred.

Refer to page 153 of [[2 - Atlas/Hobbies/A Paddler's Guide to Algonquin Park]].
