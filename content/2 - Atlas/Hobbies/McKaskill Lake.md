## **Notes** 
---
McKaskill Lake can be accessed via this route or an 8 km cart path. This route boasts great fishing and long portages. I've put the # of days as three, but it could be done in two long ones.

Refer to page 111 of [[2 - Atlas/Hobbies/A Paddler's Guide to Algonquin Park\|A Paddler's Guide to Algonquin Park]].
> Algonquin's MacKaskill Lake is an absolute gem. Each island is capped with stout white Pine. The water is sparkling turqoise, teeming with legendary brook trout to be caught. As an added bonus, there is even a historic log cabin to rent
